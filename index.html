Who we are

We're a volunteer web development service that creates websites for organizations and businesses. Our service ensures website development and hosting including with webmaster support and updates necessary.

Creating out of the ordinary designs

When was the last time you've ever seen a website with Comic Sans? Have you ever seen a website with easter eggs and flashing colors anymore? It's probably no. Here at KarloDesign, we go out of the ordinary. We provide you with creative solutions instead of just a blank website. Instead of using a CMS-based system and following their uses, create a website with creative and out of the ordinary features that many websites don't have.

Customer support

We provide customer services thru email and phone. We reply thru email at least 1 hour as soon as an email is made. Our hours of operations is also 24/7. Regular updates thru our services are at the designated hours set but we do provide immediate technical updates which are 24/7 updates.

* We use GitHub as our website hosting provider. Depending on your plan, your repository may/will be public but shouldn't affect your website performance in any way. A repository is a place that your website files will be located thru GitHub. This means your site's files and information will be made public. This isn't something to be worried about though as many websites already make their repository public to all. There isn't much of a feature to remove this but this shouldn't affect your performance of your website in any way.

* We are not an ICANN accredited business. We purchase your domain at an ICANN accredited business like Google Domains. This is also how we offer Email Forwarding for free thanks to Google Domains. Your website, if choosing Web Development and Publishing, will transfer full ownership to your own use if you wish. If you choose to continue using KarloDesign, your website files will be monitored and updated until your plan's expiry date. If you choose to stop using KarloDesign during your plan, you will not receive any reimbursement and your plan will end. Your website files will be provided to you and domain ownership and repository ownership.

* You cannot refund your payment after your purchase a plan or package. After receiving your funds, we will complete the tasks necessary and continuously assist you depending on your plan or package.
